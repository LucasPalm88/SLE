About the Team

Wearing the patriot logo on your jersey is a great honor. The culture that the patriots represent is more than just a hockey team. WE ARE A FAMILY. By being a patriot, players are expected to carry themselves with respect on and off the ice, representing what this program is all about.

Our program consists of both JV and Varsity teams, which are very competetive and strive for greatness year after year. The rink for us, is a place that you can call home.
